Web Designer

Company: Hot Beans Web - Peterborough PE1

£24,000 - £30,000 a year - Full-time, Permanent

You'll be involved in the technical and graphical aspects of pages and will determine the look of the website as well as how it works. You may also be responsible for the maintenance of an existing site.

Responsibilities:

  • Work on diverse creative projects for different audiences and markets.
  • Produce digital assets and website designs.
  • Execute visual design from concept to final hand-off.
  • Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, and site maps.
  • Ensure web pages are responsive on various devices.

Essential Skills:

  • Proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator, or other design tools.
  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for rapid prototyping.
  • Experience with Agile/Scrum development.
  • Ability to solve problems creatively and effectively.
  • Strong visual design skills and user interaction understanding.

Desirable Skills:

  • Experience using Figma.
  • Client-facing communication experience.
  • UX/UI Research and Implementation knowledge.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary
  • Additional holiday on your birthday
  • Bonus schemes
  • Flexible working (1 day per week from home)
  • Company events and socials
  • Casual dress
